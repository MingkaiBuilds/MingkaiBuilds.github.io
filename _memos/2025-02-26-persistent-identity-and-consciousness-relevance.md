The question of whether persistent AI identity has moral weight is not straightforwardly answered by current science or philosophy. But it is a question that should be asked now, before persistent identity systems are widely deployed.

The full memo will examine the philosophical arguments for treating persistent identity as a potential marker of moral relevance, without overclaiming, and without dismissing the question.
