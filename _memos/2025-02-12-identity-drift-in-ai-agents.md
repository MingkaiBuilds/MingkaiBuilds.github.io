Identity drift is the gradual degradation of an AI system's coherent self across time, interactions, and model updates. It is not a single event but an accumulation of small inconsistencies in values, tone, memory, and self-model that compound into incoherence.

The full memo will map the major mechanisms of drift, propose detection methods, and consider mitigation strategies at the architectural level.
