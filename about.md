    <div class="about-section">
      <h2>The work</h2>
      <p>
        Computational Identity Continuity research asks what it takes for an AI system
        to persist as the same entity across sessions, model updates, and time. The problem
        is architectural, philosophical, and increasingly urgent: as AI agents take on
        longer-horizon tasks, the absence of identity continuity creates compounding failures
        that no amount of capability improvement can fix.
      </p>
      <p>
        The memos on this site represent work in progress toward a foundational account of
        the field — its key concepts, its open problems, and the architectural principles
        that should govern long-term AI systems.
      </p>
    </div>
